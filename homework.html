ASCII

ASCII (/ˈæskiː/), abbreviated from American Standard Code for Information Interchange, is a character encoding standard for electronic communication.

Color

Color (American English) or colour (Commonwealth English) is the characteristic of human visual perception described through color categories, with names such as red, orange, yellow, green, blue, or purple.

Most light sources are mixtures of various wavelengths of light.

  • Additive color(RGB) is light created by mixing together light of two or more different colors. Red, green, and blue are the additive primary colors normally used in additive color systems such as projectors and computer terminals.
  • Subtractive color(CMY) uses dyes, inks, pigments, or filters to absorb some wavelengths of light and not others.

从网上下载一个BMP格式图像,用图片编辑工具另存在jpg、png、tiff格式。问三种格式中,哪种格式显示质量好?相对于BMP格式,压缩率各是多少?

BMP

  • 位深度：可选lbit、4bit、8bit及24bit
  • 压缩率：不压缩

PNG

  • 位深度：真彩色24位，外加8位表示透明
  • 压缩率：（无损）30%-50%

JPG

  • 位深度：24bit
  • 压缩率：（有损）1%-10%

tiff

  • 可变
  • 可变

综上，在JPG和PNG中比较，PNG画质更好，但压缩率较高（减少得少）。tiff由于参数可变，没法比较。
